Black rose

Black rose you are so misunderstood. People sneer at you take no notice. Cause you are blessed black rose you are unique, Don’t let them take away you diamond heart. Being a black rose it hurt sometime don’t get weary. Because your time will come don’t turn your heart Don’t walk away from a path you got to make it your own. Be a star that shines in the twilight black rose you are. A rough cut diamond don’t believe that you are not Good enough don’t allow you the spark in your eye to dim out Being a black rose it hurt sometime. Don’t get weary because your time will come. Being a black rose is a blessing walking through the fire It will mold you and make you shine like gold the pressure around you will into a beautiful pearl Being a black rose it hurt sometime Don’t get weary because your time will come Listen to me heed my words I’m a black rose dare to be you And be the black rose you are!
